Questions and Support

A good place to ask questions is the resin-interest mailing list. Not every question is answered, but many are and you can learn about clever ways of using Resin by reading it.

To subscribe, send a mail to resin-interest-request@caucho.com with the body "subscribe".

You can also subscribe to the resin-interest digest. A new digest is sent every 2 days or so. To subscribe, send a mail to resin-interest-digest-request@caucho.com with the body "subscribe".

  • Resin Platinum users will receive the highest support, including an email response in one business day and any needed bug fixes within in two weeks.
  • Registered Resin users will receive a priority for bug reports and will receive answers to simple support questions.
  • Bug reports from non-registered users are always welcome, but support questions will generally be ignored.
